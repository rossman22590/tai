About TAi

Teacher Ai (TAi) was established with the aim of providing teachers with more free time by minimizing their administrative workload. End-of-day administrative duties can be mentally taxing, so TAi was developed to enable teachers to efficiently compose emails, generate lesson ideas, and draft report card comments.

TAi utilizes the same technology as ChatGPT for generating replies. However, it's crucial to exercise your professional judgment while considering the responses you obtain, as they may not always be accurate or suitable for your specific circumstances.

Your feedback on TAi, whether positive or negative, is highly appreciated. Please feel free to leave a comment on the Contact Us page if you find it valuable or have any suggestions for new features.
