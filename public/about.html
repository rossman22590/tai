About TAi

Teacher Ai (TAi) was created with the purpose of helping teachers reduce their administrative workload and provide them with more time to focus on their students. TAi is designed to assist teachers in efficiently composing emails, generating ideas for future lessons, and drafting report card comments, all while reducing the mental strain of end-of-day administrative duties.

The underlying technology used in TAi is similar to that of ChatGPT, a state-of-the-art language model. However, it's important to exercise professional judgment when considering the responses provided by TAi as they may not always be entirely accurate or suitable for specific circumstances.

At TAi, we value feedback from our users, whether it's positive or negative. If you find TAi useful or have any suggestions for new features, please feel free to leave a comment on our Contact Us page. We're committed to continuously improving TAi to meet the evolving needs of educators.
