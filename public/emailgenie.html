EmailGenie

Quickly generate emails in a polite and professional tone.

To compose an email, choose the recipient, enter your notes in the text box (e.g. "no phones", "meeting request", "failing grade", "awesome effort today!"), and hit the blue submit button.

After composing your message, a 'Copy Text' button will appear on the page, which will automatically format the text for email and enable you to paste it directly into Gmail or Outlook etc.

Please note that if a popup appears, remember to click on 'allow' so that TeacherAI can copy and paste from the clipboard.
